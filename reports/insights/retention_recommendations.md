# Retention Recommendations

These are testable intervention hypotheses, not actions proven to prevent churn.

## Contract-upgrade incentive

- Target segment: High Risk / Priority 1
- Observed evidence: Month-to-month contract: 42.71% observed churn versus 26.54% overall.
- Suggested action: Contract-upgrade incentive
- Expected mechanism: Encourage a longer commitment while reducing avoidable switching friction.
- Suggested success metric: Contract conversion rate and 90-day retained rate

## Payment-method assistance

- Target segment: High Risk / Priority 2
- Observed evidence: Electronic-check payment: 45.29% observed churn versus 26.54% overall.
- Suggested action: Payment-method assistance
- Expected mechanism: Reduce payment-process friction and offer a more stable payment option.
- Suggested success metric: Payment-method change and 90-day retained rate

## Retention call

- Target segment: High Risk / Priority 2
- Observed evidence: Assigned from High Risk and the selected churn-probability threshold.
- Suggested action: Retention call
- Expected mechanism: Review needs and service friction with the customer.
- Suggested success metric: Contact rate and 90-day retained rate

## Onboarding support outreach

- Target segment: High Risk / Priority 2
- Observed evidence: Tenure of 12 months or less: 47.44% observed churn versus 26.54% overall.
- Suggested action: Onboarding support outreach
- Expected mechanism: Resolve early-life experience gaps before disengagement increases.
- Suggested success metric: Outreach completion and 90-day retained rate

## Technical-support outreach

- Target segment: High Risk / Priority 2
- Observed evidence: No technical-support subscription: 41.64% observed churn versus 26.54% overall.
- Suggested action: Technical-support outreach
- Expected mechanism: Reduce unresolved service and support friction.
- Suggested success metric: Support activation and 90-day retained rate

## Targeted retention email

- Target segment: Medium Risk / Priority 3
- Observed evidence: Assigned from Medium Risk and the selected churn-probability threshold.
- Suggested action: Targeted retention email
- Expected mechanism: Use a lower-cost channel to test engagement before direct outreach.
- Suggested success metric: Email engagement and 90-day retained rate

## Monitor only

- Target segment: Low Risk / Monitor
- Observed evidence: Assigned from Low Risk and the selected churn-probability threshold.
- Suggested action: Monitor only
- Expected mechanism: Observe risk movement without intervention cost.
- Suggested success metric: Risk-segment migration and observed churn rate
